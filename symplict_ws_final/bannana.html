Bananna Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 to 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar (reduce to 1/2 cup for a less sweet version)
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F) and grease a 4x8-inch loaf pan.
  2. Mash the bananas in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Mix in the melted butter with the mashed bananas.
  4. Add the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract, and mix well.
  5. Sprinkle in the baking soda and salt, and stir to combine.
  6. Add the flour and stir until just incorporated. Do not overmix.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  8. Bake for 50 to 60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Let the bread cool in the pan for a few minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Enjoy your banana bread! Feel free to add chocolate chips or nuts if desired.
