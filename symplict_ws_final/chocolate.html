Chocolate Cake Recipe

Chocolate Cake Ingredients:
  • 1 ¾ cups (220g) all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup (65g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups (400g) sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup (240ml) whole milk
  • ½ cup (120ml) vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240ml) boiling water
For the Frosting:
  • 1 cup (230g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 ½ cups (440g) powdered sugar
  • ½ cup (60g) cocoa powder
  • ½ cup (120ml) heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and sugar.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Beat with a mixer on medium speed until smooth.
  4. Carefully stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin, but that's okay!
  5. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove from pans and cool completely on a wire rack.
Frosting:
  1. In a large bowl, beat the butter on medium speed until creamy.
  2. Gradually add the powdered sugar and cocoa powder. Beat until well combined.
  3. Slowly add the heavy cream and vanilla extract, and beat until fluffy and smooth.
  4. Frost the cooled cakes, stacking them together, and cover the sides as well.

Enjoy your homemade chocolate cake!
