About Us

Welcome to our Recipe App, designed by Symplicit with love and care for the year 10 food technology students at AGGS. Our inspiration comes from a deeply personal place—one where memories of family meals and cherished recipes come together to preserve the essence of our culinary heritage.

As we grow older and begin cooking for our families, we often realize how much of our culture and identity is wrapped up in the meals we share. The fear of losing those precious recipes—like my grandmother's baked egg custard—sparked the idea for this app. It's a place where you can capture, evaluate, and share your family’s most beloved recipes, ensuring they’re never lost and always cherished.

Join us in preserving the tastes of yesterday for the cooks of tomorrow.
